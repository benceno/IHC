Social anxiety disorder costs 1 trillion USD lost in productivity per year worldwide, affecting 15 million people in the U.S.
with 28% of Americans identifying their workplace as a source of anxiety
  • We present
    A mobile app to bridge your work and social life
    Integrating an intra-company messaging system with self-created profiles and an easy-to-use notation system for recording the highs and lows of conversations
  • Daily Greetings
    Receive daily messages with helpful tips and information about the core concepts of social anxiety. Collect them and use them to improve your stress levels and social skills
  • Profiles
    Learn more about your coworkers, and track your best and worst conversations with them. Introducing yourself to a new coworker? Check out their interests for ice-breaker ideas
  • Conversations
    Chat with your coworkers, and use the in-chat notation system to quickly highlight important moments. Mark the good and bad to help you learn from your previous conversations
  • Highlights
    Keep track of the best moments in your conversations with others, so that you can always remember the high points of your socialization
  • Notes
    Jot down ideas before you forget them. Link notes with specific people's profiles, and set reminders so that you never forget what to say when it's your turn to talk

Introducing the DigiFam

A device worn around the neck that helps the elderly connect with their families.

Photo Updates

View photos posted by family members and upload your own photos to share using the DigiFam's two built-in cameras.

Video Chat with Family

Video chat with family members and experience an even split-screen design where it's easy to see both you and the family member you're chatting with.

Digital Accompaniment at the Hospital

During visits to the hospital, connect with a hospital device (such as an iPad) so that family members can accompany you digitally through a video call.

Designed for the Elderly

Minimal clutter, large fonts, text-labelled buttons and a simple interface all make for an experience tailored to the elderly.

Simple Set-Up

Setting up a family group is quick and painless - all the DigiFam needs is a family name. Joining a family group is also easy - just enter the Family ID shown on the main screen of a DigiFam that has joined a family group!

The DigiFam App

Family members or those who own a smartphone or tablet can use the DigiFam app instead of the DigiFam device. The app has all the functions of the device and can also create family groups.

Photo Updates Feature

The following images illustrate a user viewing the photo updates feature. They begin on the main screen and where they select "View Photo Updates", from there they are brought to another menu where they can choose to either "View Photos" or "Take and Upload". In this case, the user selected the first option and is brought to the Photos Feed. The last screen shows what happens when you select "Read Caption". If the user were to select the "Net" button, they would be brought to the next photo (if they are in the middle of the feed a "Previous" button would be at the top of the screen).

Why RightUp?

Posture plays an integral role in the healthy physical and mental development of a child. However, reminders and strict discipline tend to cause more harm than good, in fact creating anxiety in the child.

RightUp is a virtual reality platform that engages your children, teaching and having them model good posture. The system records your child's data and gives you in-depth diagnostics on how they and their posture are doing. From there, the system sends you additional information and instructionals on any concerns regarding your child's development, as well as any corrections you can help make.

Features

In-depth Diagnostics

Learning about posture development is easy in RightUp! The game delivers accurate diagnostics and information about your child and their healthy development. VR cameras sense the player's posture in real time, analyzing it against a model of the player's good posture.

RightUp also provides you with all you need to diagnose and address those posture problems! No time to learn while playing? Information such as back/neck issues, mental health status, and further information and exercises can all be stored or sent to your phone or email for future viewing.

Explorations and Adventures

Explore an endless world in real time as a single player or a parent-child duo! Play some games on the beach, or fight some monsters and save a town. In RightUp, you are the hero, and with your excellent bearing, there's always an adventure awaiting you!

When playing RightUp, the better posture you have, the more you can see and do in the world around you! That way, children unconsciously learn the benefits of good posture while exploring the world through the game.

Battles with Monsters

Fight to save the world of RightUp from hordes of evil monsters! And as you defeat them, gather mysterious treasures and rewards for your bravery.

When you are in a battle, the better posture you have, the more powerful you are, dealing more damage and taking less damage. Children are taught to stand straight and tall through epic battles throughout the land.

Activities and Exercises

Play a range of activities and exercises that help you stretch your body, relax your muscles and learn the importance of good posture.

Mini-games are scattered throughout the world for you to explore. Play alongside another adventure, your parent/child, or just by yourself!
